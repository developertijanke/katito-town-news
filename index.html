Former Coca-Cola Salesman Defrauds Multiple Businesses in Katito

This man was formerly employed at Katito Beverage Distributor - Coca-Cola Limited as a salesman, responsible for delivering soft beverages to both wholesalers and retailers. This week, he has defrauded more than four businesses in Katito. He falsely promises to deliver beverages and insists on payment upfront, exploiting the trust that businesses have in him due to his previous role as a Coca-Cola salesman, which he no longer holds.
